About Asurion Labs

Your phone is your inbox and connection to the people and places that matter most. With so much valuable information on your phone, you'll want to make sure it's safe—protected against threats like viruses, malware, and the loss or theft of your phone.

The comprehensive Asurion LABS app is filled with features to protect your privacy and safeguard your personal data. It gives you control over which apps can access phone contents and capabilities. And helps you locate and secure your device if it goes missing.

SECURE
  • Guard against privacy threats with anti virus protection
  • Extend battery life by identifying resource-hungry apps
  • Block viruses that can steal personal data or rack up fraudulent charges
LOCATE
  • Locate a lost phone with precise GPS technology
  • Sound an alarm from a misplaced phone, even if it's set to silent
  • Lock a missing phone to keep data safe from prying eyes
  • Remotely erase your contacts so they don’t fall into the wrong hands

If you enrolled in Mobile Care Plus, you're also covered for loss, theft, and damage and can receive a replacement device as soon as the next day. The Mobile Security app is free for Mobile Care Plus customers!
