Security

Asurion LABS features protect you and your phone from data loss, identity theft, and malicious files / apps. The Anti-Virus and Safe Browsing features ensure that the information and data you store on your handset are safe.

Manual Scan You can have Asurion LABS scan your phone for threats or malware at any time. The scan will search the files, apps, and PDFs stored on your phone.

  1. To perform a scan:
  2. Launch the Asurion LABS on your phone
  3. On the main menu, press the Security button
  4. On the Security home scree, press the Scan Phone For Threats button

Automatic Scan Asurion LABS will perform automatic threat scans on your phone based on your custom scan settings. These scans will search the files, aps and PDFs stored on your phone.

  1. To customize automatic scans:
  2. Launch the Asurion LABS on your phone
  3. On the main menu, press the Security button
  4. On the Security home screen, select a daily or weekly automatic scan schedule and a corresponding day/date. You may also select a time range when you would like the scan to occur.

Actions For Identified Threats If a threat is found during an automatic/manual sync or during an app/file download, [AppName] will notify you with information about the potential threat and provide you with the following actions:

  • Delete File: This will delete the file from your phone
  • Quarantine File: This will move the file to a safe place on your phone where it cannot cause additional harm. You can later delete or restore quarantined files.
  • Uninstall App: This will uninstall the app from your phone
  • Ignore: This will ignore the file or app in this and all future scans

Customize security settings Asurion LABS allows you to fully customize a variety of security settings from both the phone and Web:

  1. To customize your security settings from your phone:
  2. Launch the Asurion LABS on your phone.
  3. On the main menu, press the Security button.
  4. On the Security home screen, press View All Security Settings.
  5. On the Security Settings screen, you can customize the settings (see ["Security Settings"]).
  1. To customize your security settings from the Web site:
  2. Log in to LABS QA.
  3. From the dashboard, click the Security tab.
  4. Under Security Settings, click View All Security Settings.
  5. On the Security Settings screen, you can customize the settings (see ["Security Settings"]).

About security settings Asurion LABS allows you to fully customize a variety of security settings including:

  • Automatic Scans: Customize a weekly or monthly automatic threat scan schedule.
  • Automatic Updates: Update your threat protection as new viruses and malware are identified.
  • Real-Time Scans: Scan files and apps as they are downloaded.
  • Security Feedback: Display optional feedback forms to help improve security.
  • Tray Notification: Receive security notifications, such as potential threats and warnings, in your toolbar
  • Download Notification: Display privacy information when downloading apps to your phone
  • Delete Notification: Display optional feedback forms to help improve security.
  • Safe Browsing: Monitor websites for potential threats when surfing the web

Threat History Provides a list of all identified threats with dates and actions performed on those threats. Threat history can be accessed through the My Account button on the main menu.
