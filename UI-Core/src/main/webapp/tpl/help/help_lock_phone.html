Lock Phone

You have two options for securing a lost or stolen phone:

To lock a lost or stolen phone:

  1. Log on to LABS QA.
  2. Click the Location tab button at the top of the page.
  3. Click the Lock Phone button at the top of the page.
  4. To unlock your found phone, choose the appropriate option:

-Android phone- Enter your Asurion LABS password on the locked screen of your found phone.

-Blackberry phone- Enter your Blackberry phone password. (NOTE: For Blackberry phones, you must set up a password on your phone prior to using the Lock Phone feature.)

To lock a lost or stolen phone and remotely erase contacts:

  1. Log in to LABS QA.
  2. Click the Location tab button at the top of the page.
  3. Click the Erase Data button at the top of the page, which will lock the phone and erase the data. (See [Erase Data] for more details.)
  4. To unlock your found phone, choose the appropriate option:

-Android phone- Enter your Asurion LABS password on the locked screen of your found phone.

-Blackberry phone- Enter your Blackberry phone password. (NOTE: For Blackberry phones, you must set up a password on your phone prior to using the Lock Phone feature.)
