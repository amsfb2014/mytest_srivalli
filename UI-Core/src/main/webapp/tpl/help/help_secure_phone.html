Secure Phone

The Secure Phone feature allows you to sound a beeping alarm from your phone, send customized messages to a lost phone and remotely delete the contacts stored on the phone to secure your privacy.

To sound a being alarm on your phone:

  1. Click the Location tab at the top of the web page.
  2. Click the Secure Phone button at the top of the page.
  3. Click the Submit button

To send a custom message to your phone:

  1. Click the Location tab at the top of the web page.
  2. Click the Secure Phone button at the top of the page.
  3. Check the box in front of the Announce feature and enter a custom message up to 250 characters.
  4. Click the Submit button

To erase the contacts from your phone:

  1. Click the Location tab at the top of the web page.
  2. Click the Secure Phone button at the top of the page.
  3. Check the box in front of the Erase Contacts feature.
  4. Click the Submit button
