Erase Data

To erase data from your phone:

  1. Log in to LABS QA.
  2. Click the Location tab at the top of the page.
  3. Click Erase Data button at the top of the page.
  4. In the popup window, click the Erase Data & Lock Phone button.

NOTE: Only data that were previously sync-ed can be restored. The type of data that is erased varies by phone type (See [FAQ: "How do I erase data from my phone?"]).

See also:

  • * To unlock your found phone, choose the appropriate option
  • - Android phone-Enter your Asurion LABS password on the locked screen of your found phone.
  • - Blackberry phone-Enter your Blackberry phone password. (NOTE: For Blackberry phones, you must set up a password on your phone prior to using the Lock Phone feature.)
  • * To restore data to a new or found phone, see [FAQ: "How do I restore contacts to a found phone?"].
