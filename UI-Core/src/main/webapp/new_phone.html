Asurion LABS -

To update the phone displayed on the website and transfer saved data to your new phone, follow the instructions below:

First, on your current phone...

  1. Be sure to have the Asurion LABS downloaded and installed on your current phone.
  2. From either the web or the app, perform a sync to be sure you have the most recent version of your contacts saved.
  3. When the sync is complete, proceed to the next set of instructions on your new phone.

Then, on your new phone...

  1. Download Asurion LABS to your new, supported phone.
  2. Launch the Asurion LABS on your phone and complete the simple account setup.
  3. At the end of the setup process you will be prompted to 'Scan for Threats & Restore Contacts'.

    This will transfer your saved contacts to your new phone. You may need to refresh the website to see your new phone listed.
