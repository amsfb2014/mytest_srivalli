Asurion Mobile has implemented measures to help protect against the unauthorized access, loss, misuse and alteration of the Archived Data and Your Personal Information. After Mobile Security is installed, the Archived Data and Your Personal Information generally is encrypted before being transmitted over the Internet to Asurion Mobile. The Archived Data and Your Personal Information will be encrypted by Your phone before it is transferred to Asurion Mobile Storage Systems.
