Locate Phone

To locate a lost device using GPS:

  1. Click the Locate Phone button at the top of the Location page.
  2. The phone location will be updated on the Location page if your phone is turned on and within coverage area.

Remember: Safety first. Asurion recommends you do not attempt to retrieve your device from any location that is not safe, that you do not recognize, or that is not familiar to you. If you believe your device has been stolen or is in an unsafe or unfamiliar location, you should contact your local law enforcement.

Location settings

Asurion Labs will perform automatic location checks based on your custom settings and save that location online in case you ever lose your phone.

To change your location settings:

  1. Tap the Settings link at the top of the page.
  2. You may customize you location check frequency as well as set a minimum battery level at which automatic location checks will stop to conserve battery life.

Location accuracy

To ensure the most accurate locations possible, make sure all Location Services (GPS, etc.) settings on your phone are set to ON.

To check Location Services settings:

  1. Launch the Asurion LABS Application on your phone.
  2. On the main menu, press the Location button.
  3. At the top of the screen, icons indicate whether each location setting is ON (green checkmark) or OFF (warning icon). You can click any Location Services setting to turn it ON or OFF.
