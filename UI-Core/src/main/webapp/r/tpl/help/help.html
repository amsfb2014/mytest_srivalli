Locate Phone

There are two options for finding a lost phone:

To find a misplaced phone nearby, sound a phone alarm:

  1. Log in to LABS QA.
  2. Click the Sound Alarm button at the top of the page.
  3. In the popup window, click the Sound Alarm button.
  4. To disarm the alarm, press Stop Alarm button on the device or press the volume on the side of your device up or down.

To find a lost or stolen device using GPS technology:

  1. Log in to LABS QA.
  2. Click the Locate Phone button at the top of the Location page.
  3. In the popup window, click the Locate Phone button.
  4. The phone location will be updated on the Location page.

Remember: Safety first. Asurion recommends you do not attempt to retrieve your device from any location that is not safe, that you do not recognize, or that is not familiar to you. If you believe your device has been stolen or is in an unsafe or unfamiliar location, you should contact your local law enforcement.

Location settings

Asurion LABS allows you to find a lost phone on a map, using the phone's Location Services, such as GPS technology. In addition to the Locate feature, the Asurion LABS Application Web site provides a complete set of recovery features including Sound Alarm, Lock Phone and Erase Contacts. Visit LABS QA to find and secure a lost phone.

To change your location settings:

  1. Launch the Asurion LABS Application on your phone.
  2. On the main menu, press the Location button.
  3. On the Location home screen, press the Location Settings button.
  4. Select a frequency for the automatic location check schedule.

Asurion LABS will perform automatic location checks based on your custom settings and save that location online at LABS QA.

Location accuracy

Location accuracy is determined by a number of factors, including things like proximity to a cell tower, signal strength, and the phoneâ€™s current location (GPS reading are more accurate if your phone is outdoors). To ensure the most accurate locations possible, make sure all Location Services (GPS, etc.) settings on your phone are set to ON.

To check Location Services settings:

  1. Launch the Asurion LABS Application on your phone.
  2. On the main menu, press the Location button.
  3. At the top of the screen, icons indicate whether each location setting is ON (green checkmark) or OFF (warning icon). You can click any Location Services setting to turn it ON or OFF.
