Safe Browsing

Safe browsing helps to protect your personal and information by blocking sites that may be potentially harmful or malicious. For example, these may be sites that are masquerading as online shops or banks in an attempt to steal your personal or financial information.

  1. To enable safe browsing:
  2. Launch the > Asurion LABS on your phone
  3. On the main menu, press the Safe Browsing button
  4. On the Safe Browsing home screen, check the Safe Browsing On checkbox.
