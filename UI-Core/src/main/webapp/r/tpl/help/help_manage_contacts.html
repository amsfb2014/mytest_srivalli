Manage Data from the Web

Add contacts

  1. Click the Backup tab at the top of the web page.
  2. Click Create New Contact(s) in the Manage Contacts tab on the left side of the screen.
  3. Enter new contact information and click Save.

Edit Contacts:

  1. Click the Backup tab at the top of the web page.
  2. Select the contact to edit.
  3. Click the Edit Contact button in the contact details section on the right side of the page.
  4. Enter edits for the selected contact and click Save.

Delete Contacts:

From the Web site, you can delete contacts one at a time, or in batches:

To delete a single contact:

  1. Click the Backup tab at the top of the web page.
  2. Select the contact to delete.
  3. Click the Delete This Contact From Phone & Web button in the contact details section on the right side of the page.

To delete multiple contacts:

  1. Click the Backup tab at the top of the web page.
  2. Click the checkbox next to each contact to delete, and then click Delete Contact(s) on the Manage Contacts panel on the left side of the page.

Delete contacts vs Remove contacts from phone

You can delete contacts from both phone and Web site, or you can remove them from just the phone and maintain them on the Web site only.

  • • Delete contacts - Removes the contacts from both the phone and the website. You can do this only on the website.
  • • Remove contacts from phone -- Removes the contacts from your phone. A copy of it will be saved on the website but set to not sync with your phone. If you want to permanently delete the contact, use the delete contact option.

To remove a contact:

  1. Click the Backup tab at the top of the web page.
  • • Select the checkbox next to the contact, and Click the Remove From Phone button to remove the contact from just the phone, and store it on the Web site.

Note: Contacts to be synced with your phone will have the On Phone text under their names. You may perform the same action but choose Add To Phone to reverse the process.

Import contacts:

  1. Click the Backup tab at the top of the web page.
  2. Click the Import Contact link in the Manage Contacts panel on the left side of the page, and follow the instructions provided.

Export contacts:

  1. Click the Backup tab at the top of the web page.
  2. Click the Export contacts link in the Manage Contacts panel on the left side of the page, and follow the instructions provided.

Print contacts:

  1. Click the Backup tab at the top of the web page.
  2. Click the Print Contacts link in the Manage Contacts panel on the left side of the page.
  3. Select File -> Print or hit Ctrl+P to print the page..

Restore Data from Trash

  1. Click the Backup tab at the top of the web page.
  2. Click the Trash tab at the top of the page.
  3. Select a contact and press the Restore Contact button in the contact details, or click Restore All Contacts in the left panel.
