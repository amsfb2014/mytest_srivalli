Diagnostics

Diagnostic Scan analyzes your device's use of battery, memory and file storage resources, and identifies the apps and files that most affect device performance.

With this information, and a set of easy-to-use tools, users can improve battery life and device speed, and avoid errors due to insufficient storage capacity.

For help with these features, and many other questions about how to get the most from your device, the service also includes free assistance from our customer service technicians - just a call away
