Transfer Data

To transfer data from one supported phone to another:

To transfer contacts between phones:

  1. You must first have the Asurion Labs app launched on your old phone. Launch the app on your old phone and perform a backup from the main menu to ensure you have the latest version of your data backed up.
  2. Download Asurion Labs to your new phone and complete the install process.
  3. At the end of the installation process, choose the Restore Data option to complete the transfer to your new phone.
